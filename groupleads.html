FAQ

Common questions from agencies.

What counts as a lead?

A real reply conversation where a business contact shows interest, curiosity, or openness to reviewing group coverage options.

Do I pay for bounced emails?

No. Bounces, auto-replies, unsubscribes, rejections, and responses without intent do not count as qualified leads.

Can I choose the state or industries?

Yes. Campaigns can be segmented by state, city, industry, business type, and other practical targeting filters.

How are leads delivered?

Positive replies can be forwarded, introduced by CC, or tracked in a client dashboard depending on your preference.

Who is this for?

Insurance agencies, benefits brokers, group health producers, employee benefits consultants, and small group specialists.

Is this for consumer insurance leads?

No. SimplyBooked focuses on small business and group benefits opportunities, not individual consumer insurance leads.
